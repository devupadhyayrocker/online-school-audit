Overview

The audits are an integral part of our “Improvement cycle” to stimulate quality work and to implement a quality review. It provides us with an opportunity of self assessment based on enhancement and realization of goals set by the CO. Therefore, it is a process of mentoring and not monitoring.

With our team’s professional judgment, sufficient and appropriate audit procedures were completed and evidence gathered to support the accuracy of conclusions reached and contained in this report. The conclusions are based on a comparison of situations and assessments as they existed at the time of the audit with the established criteria.

Objectives of the Audit

  • To observe and analyze the school’s potential to deliver optimal learning experience
  • To identify the learning strategies designed and executed during the classroom practices by the teachers
  • To analyse the strength and weakness of the classroom instruction
  • To observe the implementation of safe, fair and constructive classroom management
  • To analyse the peer's understanding
  • To analyse what Staff think about themselves
  • To gauge the involvement of the teachers and Staff
  • To observe that the implementation of the curriculum is not just in practice, but also in spirit
Audit Introduction

I welcome you to the first of its kind - school audit online platform – JAIPURIA AUDIT PLATFORM FOR PERFOMANCE. During this pandemic when the travel has been restricted, Corporate Office in quick time conceptualized the platform. Audit is a process which enables the management of the school to check compliances and an opportunity to ensure that the people are engaged to implement the right processes.

Seth Anandram Jaipuria School Corporate office conducted a 360 degree audit of the Staff for the session 2020-21. The aim of this audit was to examine the school holistically and evaluate the processes in all departments. The audit processes have been created for assessing the effectiveness of curriculum implementation, ascertain Staff effectiveness, maintenance of routine academic and non academic processes and their behavioral aspects.

Jaipuria Corporate office team thanks the school stakeholders for conducting the smooth audit and wish the best for overcoming the gaps with our recommendations and constant supervision in the coming years.
